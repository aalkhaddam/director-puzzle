Instructions

• This is a riddle/puzzle/escape room game (that will hopefully take longer than 15 minutes).
• Each clue will either be something your team will need to search on the UofT/OISE website, a fun riddle, or something you might need to find creatively.
• Once you get to a puzzle, your team simply enter what you think the answer is - word, number, phrase.
• After a successful answer, your team will receive an alert that includes a letter; the letters collected from all correct answers will spell out a word. BE CAREFUL! The word may be an anagram and have more than one answer.
• If your team gets stuck, a SKIP button will be available BUT your team won't receive a letter (if everyone is stuck though, I will provide the answer).
• Once you begin, your time starts and will be displayed after enterring the final word. Fastest time gets bragging rights (or we can decide on a prize).
• Most importantly, have fun!
